## Design Explanation

### Design story

Nympha draws from the gentle curvature of monsoon leaves — forms that hold water and redirect light. The sconce's sculpted petal shape wraps a warm LED source, directing all light upward and outward for a soft, glare-free ambient glow. The natural timber veneer adds organic warmth.

### Materials & finish

- Body: Die-cast aluminum (powder coat black, white, or polished brass)
- Accent: Sustainable timber veneer (walnut for black/brass bodies, oak for white body)
- Diffuser: Opal polycarbonate (concealed within the petal fold)
- Mounting: Powder-coated steel wall bracket with concealed screws

### How the form works

The LED module is recessed within the fold of the leaf form. Light fires upward onto the painted interior surface of the upper petal, then reflects outward as a soft wall wash. The timber veneer on the lower face catches a subtle glow spill, revealing the grain. No direct LED exposure — zero glare.

### Best suited for

- Hotel corridors and guest rooms
- Restaurant intimate seating areas
- Residential living rooms and bedrooms
- Spa and wellness reception areas
- Reading nooks and lounge corners

### Differentiators vs similar lights

- Single-piece cast aluminum body — no visible seams
- Real timber veneer, not printed or simulated
- IP44 rated — suitable for covered outdoor and high-humidity spaces
- Interchangeable petal sizes on same wall bracket
