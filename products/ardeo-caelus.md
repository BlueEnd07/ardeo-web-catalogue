## Design Explanation

### Design story

Caelus is inspired by the concentric rings of Saturn and the layered orbits of celestial bodies. The pendant arranges multiple illuminated rings at staggered heights, creating a floating, weightless composition that draws the eye upward.

### Materials & finish

- Frame: Extruded brushed aluminum (black or white powder coat)
- Diffuser: Opal acrylic with 92% light transmission for uniform glow
- Cable: Adjustable stainless steel suspension cables with white textile covering
- Canopy: Low-profile white or black round ceiling canopy

### How the form works

Each ring houses an internal LED strip that fires inward, bouncing light off a curved reflector and through the opal diffuser. The staggered suspension creates visual depth — rings appear to float independently while forming a cohesive whole. Tunable white (2700K–4000K) allows the mood to shift from warm ambient to cool task.

### Best suited for

- Hotel lobbies and atriums
- Restaurant dining areas
- High-end retail
- Corporate reception areas
- Large residential entryways and living rooms

### Differentiators vs similar lights

- True tunable white across 2700K–4000K (competitors often offer fixed CCT)
- Staggered ring heights create 3D depth, not a flat ring arrangement
- Opal acrylic diffuser eliminates visible LED spot
- Tool-less height adjustment for each ring
