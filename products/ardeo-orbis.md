## Design Explanation

### Design story

Orbis is engineered for the architect who demands light precision without visual noise. The name comes from the Latin for "circle" or "orbit" — reflecting the pure circular form and the precise orbital path of its internal optics.

### Materials & finish

- Housing: Die-cast aluminum with heat sink fins (invisible above ceiling)
- Trim: Precision-machined aluminum, powder-coated matte white or matte black
- Reflector: Micro-prismatic high-purity aluminum (95% reflectivity)
- Lens: Interchangeable optical module — snap-in 25° (spot) or 40° (flood)
- Spring clips: Stainless steel for corrosion resistance

### How the form works

The COB LED fires into a TIR (total internal reflection) optic, producing a sharp cutoff with minimal glare. The micro-prismatic reflector smooths the beam edge for a uniform pool of light. The trim sits flush with the ceiling — a 1mm reveal gap eliminates ceiling shadow. The optical module is tool-free interchangeable from below the ceiling.

### Beam angle & light spread

| Size | 25° Spot — throw at 3m | 40° Flood — pool at 3m |
|---|---|---|
| 4" | Ø1.3m, 320 lux | Ø2.2m, 180 lux |
| 5" | Ø1.3m, 520 lux | Ø2.2m, 290 lux |
| 6" | Ø1.3m, 710 lux | Ø2.2m, 400 lux |

### Best suited for

- Retail accent lighting
- Gallery and museum lighting
- High-end residential
- Corporate offices
- Hospitality (lobbies, corridors, restaurants)

### Differentiators vs similar lights

- Tool-free beam angle swap (competitors require reflector replacement)
- 95+ CRI across all CCTs (no CRI drop at 2700K)
- Fully integrated heat sink — no driver box above ceiling
- 0–100% flicker-free dimming
- Trim gap precisely controlled — no ugly ceiling shadows
